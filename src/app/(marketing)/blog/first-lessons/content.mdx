# Tips for Your First Lessons

Starting an instrument is like meeting a new friend: go slow, be curious, and enjoy the sound.

## 1. Tiny Sessions

Begin with 10–15 minutes a day. We build consistency first, then duration.

## 2. The “3‑Step Loop”

1. Breathe and listen to a reference note  
2. Play once, slowly  
3. Reflect: what improved? one small tweak for the next try

> Progress loves clarity. One small win per day beats long, sporadic cramming.

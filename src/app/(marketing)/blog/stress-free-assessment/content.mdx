# Stress‑Free Music Assessment

Assessment can be musical and humane:

- **Micro‑rubrics**: tone, rhythm, posture, expression (1–3 smiley scale).
- **Model + Mirror**: demonstrate, record student, reflect together.
- **Celebrate patterns**: “You fixed the last shift! Let's keep that recipe.”

> Replace “pass/fail” with “before/after”. Progress is the performance.
